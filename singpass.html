Singpass app 2.0


Redesign the 3 year old Singpass app to scale for future growth, and improve visual design of the app

Overview

The Singpass app is Singapore's National Digital Identity platform with over 3.5 million users. With this app, users are able to prove their personal identity and make secured transactions with over 340 government agencies and private sector services.
