Improving the LiveMore Track experience


Design the end-to-end Track experience to improve track completion rate


Role: Researcher and Designer (Solo)

About LiveMore

LiveMore is a free self-development app providing content from experts worldwide to help people implement positive changes to their lives. We provide 3 types of content - namely Short Reads, Quizzes and Tracks.

We were facing low completion rate for Tracks - which were guided exercises that take 20-30 minutes to be completed.

Based on our data from Mixpanel, we had a completed track rate of merely 1% in March 2020.

Interaction and Design - make minimal changes to improve usability

Update: Reduce the need for users to tap on "Continue" repeatedly after each exercise. Append different exercises into 1 page, and the "Continue" button only appears after the user has completed 1 Action with several Exercises. A page progress indicator is also added so that the user has clear feedback on where his/her progress is at within the page.

Conclusion

2 months after the Tracks improvements were made, I was glad to see that the completion of tracks had improved. Of course, there were still many aspects we could work on within LiveMore Tracks.
